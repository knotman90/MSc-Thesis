Complex phenomena modellation with cellular automata

Complex phenomena are different from models like Boltzmann lattice model because of the lager scale of the space that they take into account, like lava flow simulation models that evolve on a topographical mountain region map 79. CA, as defined in the previous chapter are already suitable for this kind of modeling but, an extension was introduced by [@Gregorio1999] to better fit the problem of complex phenomena modellation.
