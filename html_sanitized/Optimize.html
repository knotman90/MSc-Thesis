Optimize

After each round of parallelization the developer can think and move to further optimize the application. Optimizations can be applied incrementally, and can be tested in term of speedup achieved and validated. Optimization can be applied at different level: from the overlapping of memory transfers with computation to the best block-grid dimensions for a given kernel in order to augment the occupancy. Profiling and debug tools are invaluable for this stage of the process as they can suggest the next-best course of action and provide vital information about the performances.
