Outflows computation

In “flow computing mode”, minimizing outflows, \(\phi_{(0,i)}\), are re-computed by considering the new CA clock \(\overline{t}\). Subsequently, lava outflows, \(h_{(0,i)}\), are computed proportionally to the displacement, by simply multiplying the minimizing outflow by the ratio between the actual displacement and the maximum allowed: \[h_{(0,i)} = \phi_{(0,i)} \frac{ s_{(0,i)} }{ w }\] Final velocity and displacement are computed as in Section [sec:min-ouflows].
