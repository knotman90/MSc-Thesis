Elementary process \(\tau_2\): updating of mass and momentum

The elementary process updates lava thickness and momentum. It is formally defined as: \[\tau_2: Q_f^9 \times Q_{\overrightarrow{v_f}}^9 \to Q_h \times Q_{\overrightarrow{p}}\] Once the outflows \(h_{(0,i)}\) are known for each cell \(c \in R\), the new lava thickness inside the cell can be obtained by considering the mass balance between inflows and outflows: \[h_{(0)} = \sum_{i=0}^9 (h_{(i,0)} - h_{(0,i)})\]

Moreover, also the new value for the momentum can be updated by accumulating the contributions given by the inflows: \[\overrightarrow{p}_{(0)} = \sum_{i=0}^9 h_{(i,0)} \overrightarrow{v_f}_{_{(i,0)}}\]
